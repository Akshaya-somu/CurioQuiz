About CurioQuiz

An MCQ generation system that uses NLP to create educational assessment questions from input text.

Fast Generation

Generate multiple-choice questions instantly from your educational content, saving hours of manual work.

Intelligent Analysis

Our NLP algorithms identify key concepts and extract the most important information for creating relevant questions.

Editorial Results

Not completely satisfied with a generated question? Edit any part of the question or options to refine it to your needs.
